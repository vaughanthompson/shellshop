Destinations and timing

We deliver to New Zealand addresses (North Is, South Is, Stewart Is.) only.

We will dispatch Products as soon as possible after your order is completed. Allow 1 - 5 business days for local (New Zealand) delivery. Rural addresses take an additional 2 days.

We reserve the right to alter permitted shipping addresses at any time without notice.

Shipping and freight charges are listed on this page. We reserve the right to alter permitted shipping and freight charges at any time, and any new charges will apply to all orders placed after the altered charges are listed on this webpage- or to existing orders where circumstances change dramatically- ie, requesting an urgent / expedited delivery, or changing the final destination of an order prior to it's dispatch.

Freight Charges

NZ Residential shipping is nationwide including major metro and most NZ towns and cities. NZ Rural covers all (RD) regional areas including Stewart Island.

Freight is calculated on the combined cubic metre (m3) size of products in your order. The current freight charge is: $7.50 per 0.03m3.

Your credit card is processed via the Stripe payment gateway and is protected via Stripe's secure SSL as well as being encrypted on your browser using Stripe's client side encryption. For added security the entire payment page is also secured using Microsoft Azure's SSL with an Azure SSL certificate. The website is hosted on Microsoft Azure which is a secure, PCI compliant hosting environment.
